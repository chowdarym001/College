What skills will I gain?

  • Familiarity with healthcare settings

    We hold clinicals in different settings to ensure you’re comfortable, knowledgeable, and adaptable—wherever you’re working.

  • Hands-on clinical training

    Providing preventative, therapeutic and maintenance care to patients demands your education be hands-on. That’s exactly what you can expect.

  • Basic patient care

    You’ll become proficient in taking temperatures and blood pressures and dressing wounds—key foundational nursing skills.

  • Expertise in gathering health data

    Knowing how to properly observe patients and chart and report changes in their condition is another reason LPNs are critical to patient care.
