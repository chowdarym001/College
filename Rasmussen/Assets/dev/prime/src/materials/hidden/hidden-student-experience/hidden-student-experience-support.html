Success is easier with all this support.

  • Personal Advisor

    Your advisor is your personal guide throughout your Rasmussen College experience. From course scheduling to financial aid to learning support, this is your point person.

  • Experienced Faculty

    An inspiring teacher is someone you never forget. Learn from passionate faculty who have years of front-line experience in your field of study.

  • 24/7 Digital Support

    We take technical help seriously. Whether you need help locating a faculty member’s email address or assistance troubleshooting internet connectivity, tur to us.

  • Online Learning Center

    Convenient and accessible tutoring can be a lifesaver. Especially when it’s at your fingertips. Besides access to faculty mentoring, this is where you’ll find study aids, writing assistance, test-taking strategies, time management tips and more.

  • Materials Included

    With a few select exceptions, all course materials are included in the total course cost to reduce surprises down the road. What’s more, since all assigned books are e-books, there’s just a convenient link.

  • Career Support

    From early guidance setting your career path to effective resume writing and interview coaching, we have tools along the way to help you successfully enter the job market.
