Competency-based education

Competency-based education (CBE) is an innovative way of learning that allows you to leverage prior, relevant experience to move more quickly through content you’re already familiar with. Better still, you’re able to complete these real-world projects at your own pace.

Self-directed assessments

Students take these interactive, self-directed assessments independently online, with access to academic assistance, if needed. Assessments are per attempt and can help you save even more on tuition.
