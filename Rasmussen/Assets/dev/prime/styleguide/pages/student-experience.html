Student Experience

Here, your student experience is uniquely your own.

At Rasmussen, students have a choice in their learning. Since you know how you learn best, we offer options, backing them up with student services to help along the way. Based on your program, you can learn in online courses, traditional classrooms, at your own pace or following an instructors set schedule. Courses can include hands-on experience in virtual labs, project assessments and interactive sessions with experienced faculty. This flexibility helps each of our students have the college experience thats best for them.

Success is easier with all this support.

  • Personal Advisor

    Your advisor is your personal guide throughout your Rasmussen College experience. From course scheduling to financial aid to learning support, this is your point person.

  • Experienced Faculty

    An inspiring teacher is someone you never forget. Learn from passionate faculty who have years of front-line experience in your field of study.

  • 24/7 Digital Support

    We take technical help seriously. Whether you need help locating a faculty members email address or assistance troubleshooting internet connectivity, tur to us.

  • Online Learning Center

    Convenient and accessible tutoring can be a lifesaver. Especially when its at your fingertips. Besides access to faculty mentoring, this is where youll find study aids, writing assistance, test-taking strategies, time management tips and more.

  • Materials Included

    With a few select exceptions, all course materials are included in the total course cost to reduce surprises down the road. Whats more, since all assigned books are e-books, theres just a convenient link.

  • Career Support

    From early guidance setting your career path to effective resume writing and interview coaching, we have tools along the way to help you successfully enter the job market.

Our respected degrees delivered your way.

Courses are either all online or a blend of online and on campus. The flexible online component is crucial for most of our students and even fully online students have access to all campus offerings. On-campus courses offer hands-on instruction and carry the extra benefit of one-on-one instruction with other students and faculty.

These Flex Choice options could save you time and money.

Competency-based education

Competency-based education (CBE) is an innovative way of learning that allows you to leverage prior, relevant experience to move more quickly through content youre already familiar with. Better still, youre able to complete these real-world projects at your own pace.

Self-directed assessments

Students take these interactive, self-directed assessments independently online, with access to academic assistance, if needed. Assessments are per attempt and can help you save even more on tuition.
