Practical Nursing (LPN)

A daily caregiver’s work is critical—and a privilege. In as few as 12 months1, you can earn your Practical Nursing Diploma, be prepared to sit for the NCLEX-PN exam2 and start your career as a licensed practical nurse. Get started now in the career you’ve imagined.

  • Start soon—finish strong

    Four calendar starts a year, no prerequisites and no wait list means whenever you’re ready to start your education, a new class of students is getting ready to begin the adventure with you.

  • Variety of settings

    Because an LPN is qualified to work in a variety of settings, we take you there first. Our clinicals let you experience various work environments before earning your diploma.

  • Prepare to pass

    From your very first day in class, our program is designed to equip you with the knowledge and skills to confidently sit for the NCLEX-PN exam.

  • Flex Choice® = self-paced courses

    Nursing students can take advantage of an innovative way of learning. Combining traditional courses with self-directed assessments can decrease program costs and put more flexibility into your schedule.
