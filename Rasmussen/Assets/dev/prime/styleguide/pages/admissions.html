Frequently Asked Questions

Cost/Funding

How much is tuition?

A good place to start is our tuition estimator, found on the Tuition page. Just fill in the required fields for your tuition estimate. For more specific inquiries, speak with a program manager.

What are financial aid options?

Our Tuition page outlines some financial aid options. As part of the enrollment process, your program manager will walk you through an overview of financial aid and what type of aid may be specifically suited to you.

Will financial aid cover my tuition?

You’ll find an overview of financial aid on our Tuition page. For more specific questions, see your program manager.

What is a Transfer of Credit?

Transfer of credit is credit for previously completed college courses you have taken. For questions, reach out to work a program manager who can guide you on collecting the required information needed to take this step.

What is the Rasmussen College School Code for FAFSA?

To look up a school code, visit the online FAFSA application. Connect with a program manager if you are unsure on what code to use. Codes are dependent on location and program.

Program/Classes

Is Rasmussen an accredited college?

Rasmussen College is regionally accredited by the Higher Learning Commission. For more information about other accreditation within certain programs, visit our Degrees page.

What will my class schedule be?

As a student, you will work with your program manager and an advisor to create your class schedule. For a high-level view on courses in a specific program, visit the course catalog.

What does a full time schedule look like?

As a student, you will work with a program manager and an advisor to create a schedule that works best for you. Class schedules vary by program and credits per course.
